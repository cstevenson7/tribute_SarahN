Sarah Hueniken

North American Lead Difficulty Ice Champion 2014-2016

ACMG Alpine and Rock Guide, Professional Climber

10 things you didn't know about Sarah Hueniken
  • First North American woman to climb M11, M12, M13 and M14.
  • Rock climb Redpoint, 5.13c.
  • Alpine examiner for the ACMG.
  • First ascent team to climb Niagara Falls with Will Gadd.
  • She is currently one of only 10 women to hold the ACMG ALpine and Rock certification in Canada.
  • She runs women-specific rock, ice and alpine camps every year with the emphasis on building the confidence and judgment required to be out front.
  • Ice and mixed climbing fill her winters and rock and alpine adventures compose the summer months.
  • She is married to Will Gadd who is also an ACMG Alpine and Rock guide.
  • She lives in Canmore, Alberta and knew at 15 that she wanted to live and work in the mountains.
  • She also lived Wyoming where she worked as a mountain instructor for NOLS,an climbed in amazing places like Red Rocks, Devils Tower,Cochise and the City of Rocks.
My passion for climbing consumed me.

- Sarah Hueniken
